The time machine to Sheffields Past

kelham Island Museum is a beautifully maintained step into the past of Sheffield, containing pieces from when the city was a steel works and even the Second World War.

Rediscover almost forgotten parts of Sheffield and get a first hand look at how they used to work for yourself.

Why Visit

You should certainly visit if you are interested in sheffields history, whether the steel works or what we did in the war. We have our own on-site cafe which sells an assortment of food and drink.

Due to the free price of a visit, it is perfect for last minute outings as well as being interesting to those keen to learn the history of Sheffield
