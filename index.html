The time machine to Sheffields Past

kelham Island Museum is a beautifully maintained step into the past of Sheffield, containing pieces from when the city was a steel works and even the Second World War.

Where is Kelham Island?

A short tram journey and walk from the City Centre will bring you to the Island and Museum.

Why Visit

You should certainly visit if you are interested in sheffields history, whether the steel works or what we did in the war. We have our own on-site cafe
